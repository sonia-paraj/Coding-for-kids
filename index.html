About Us

Teaching Tech Skills to the Next Generation

Coding for Kids is a dedicated computer education institute in Ghevra Village, Delhi. We believe every child deserves to understand and use technology — not just as a user, but as a creator and innovator.

🎯

Our Mission

To teach coding, AI, and digital skills to kids in the simplest, most engaging way — making technology accessible to every child in Delhi.
